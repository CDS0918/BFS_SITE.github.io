Welcome to BFS Agriculture

At BFS Agriculture, we are dedicated to providing top-tier agri services and custom farming solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of modern agriculture. Whether you're a large-scale producer or a landowner looking to maximize your investment, our expertise and commitment to excellence ensure success. We leverage innovative practices and cutting-edge technology to foster productivity, sustainability, and profitability across all our operations.

Explore our specialized services designed to support every aspect of your agricultural endeavors.

Farm to Farm Services

Optimizing operations for producers, by producers. Our comprehensive farm-to-farm services are designed to enhance efficiency, yield, and overall farm performance. We offer flexible solutions that integrate seamlessly with your existing practices.

Custom Planting & Seeding

Precision planting services for various crops, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment for optimal seed placement and emergence.

Harvesting Solutions

Efficient and timely harvesting services to maximize crop recovery and minimize losses, adapted to different crop types and field conditions.

Field Cultivation & Tillage

Expert land preparation, including conventional and conservation tillage, to create ideal seedbeds and improve soil health.

Spraying & Application Services

Accurate and effective application of fertilizers, pesticides, and herbicides with modern spraying technology to protect your crops and enhance growth.

Drainage & Land Improvement

Installation and maintenance of drainage systems, tiling, and other land improvement services to optimize water management and land productivity.

Cover Cropping & Soil Health

Consultation and implementation of cover cropping strategies to improve soil structure, fertility, and reduce erosion.

Farm to Owner Services

For landowners seeking professional management and strategic development of their agricultural assets. We provide tailored solutions that ensure your land is productive, profitable, and well-maintained without the day-to-day operational burden.

Land Management & Lease Agreements

Comprehensive management of agricultural land, including tenant sourcing, lease negotiation, and ongoing property oversight.

Crop Share & Cash Rent Management

Professional administration of crop share and cash rent agreements to optimize returns and ensure fair partnerships.

Agronomic Consultation

Expert advice on crop selection, rotation, soil testing, and nutrient management to maximize land potential and sustainability.

Financial Reporting & Analysis

Detailed financial tracking, budgeting, and performance analysis to keep you informed about your investment's profitability.

Conservation & Environmental Planning

Development and implementation of conservation plans, waterway maintenance, and environmental stewardship programs.

Farm Infrastructure Development

Planning and oversight of improvements to farm infrastructure, such as barns, irrigation systems, and fencing.
