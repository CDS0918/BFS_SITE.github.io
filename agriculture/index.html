About BFS Agriculture

BFS Agriculture is the operations branch of a multi-generational farm based in Central Pennsylvania. We currently manage over 750 acres of row crops, producing corn, soybeans, wheat, and a variety of cover and forage crops. These crops support our sister dairy and beef operations while also supplying high-quality commodities to local food manufacturers.

As we transition to the next generation of leadership, our mission is to expand our management area and serve as a trusted partner to help landowners and producers get the most value and productivity from their farms. We are committed to land stewardship, regenerative practices, and the use of modern agronomic technologies.

Core Management Services
