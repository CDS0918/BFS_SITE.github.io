About BFS Agriculture

[cite_start]

BFS Agriculture is the operations branch of a **multi-generational farm** based in Central Pennsylvania[cite: 6, 19]. [cite_start]We currently manage **over 750 acres of row crops** [cite: 7][cite_start], producing **corn, soybeans, wheat**, and a variety of **cover and forage crops**[cite: 7]. [cite_start]These crops support our sister dairy and beef operations while also supplying high-quality commodities to local food manufacturers[cite: 8].
