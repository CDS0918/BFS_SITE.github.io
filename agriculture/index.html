About BFS Agriculture

BFS Agriculture is a multi-generational farm based in Central Pennsylvania. We currently manage 1000+ acres for 10+ clients, producing corn, soybeans, wheat, and a variety of cover and forage crops.

Our mission is serve as a trusted partner to help landowners and producers get the most value from their farms through quality inputs, clear data, and optimized practices.

Core Commitments:

  • ✔ Regenerative soil practices
  • ✔ Modern agronomic technologies
